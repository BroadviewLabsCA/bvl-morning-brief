BVL Prompt of the Day▼

Turn a client brief into an AI-search-optimized content outline

Use this when starting a new content project for a pharma or health client:

> "You are a healthcare content strategist. A patient or HCP searches [condition/treatment] in an AI-powered search engine (ChatGPT, Gemini, Perplexity). Based on what AI ttols look for when surfacing authoritative health content — clinical accuracy, clear structure, source credability — give me a 5-section content outline for [topic] that would be cited in an AI-generated answer. For each section, explain what makes it credible and citable."

Use it to pressure-test whether your content brief will survive AI search — not just Google.

RED-C Phase III Failure (January 23, 2026)

Bausch Health's bid to extend the Xifaxan franchise via rifaximin SSD failed. Both RED-C-3131 and RED-C-3132 trials missed their primary endpoint: delaying first-onset HE in cirrhosis patients with no prior HE history. 1,000+ patients, 398 sites, 17 countries. Well-tolerated but not effective vs. placebo. CEO Tom Appio reviewing full dataset. This leaves Xifaxan without a pipeline successor past the patent cliff.

  • Source: Bausch Health RED-C Update – PR Newswire ↗
  • Source: Clinical Trials Arena – Rifaximin SSD Phase III Miss ↗

2026 ACG HE Guidelines — Positive for Xifaxan

Published March 2026. Strong recommendation (high certainty) for rifaximin addition in recurrent OHE. Conditional recommendation for outpatient prevention. Recommended 14 days pre-TIPS and 6+ months post-TIPS. Reinforces existing indications; does not expand into primary prevention.
