## Strategic Signals

1. **Antibiotic stewardship is becoming a brand liability risk.** The emerging narrative around Arestin overuse — combined with broader dental antibiotic scrutiny — means OraPharma will need proactive "appropriate use" messaging. For BVL work, any HCP-facing content should reinforce clinical criteria for Arestin use (pocket depth thresholds, adjunctive SRP context) rather than broad-use encouragement. A stewardship-aligned positioning could actually differentiate Arestin as the *responsible* choice vs. generic alternatives.

2. **Canada/Puerto Rico expansion creates new audience segments.** If BVL has any role in the Arestin account, the geographic rollout into Canada opens a bilingual (English/French) market with distinct regulatory and professional society dynamics (RCDSO, CDHO vs. US state boards). Puerto Rico adds a Spanish-language HCP audience. Worth scoping whether existing creative assets require localization or whether new market launch support is needed.
