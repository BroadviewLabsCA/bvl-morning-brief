**RED-C Phase III Failure (January 23, 2026)**
Bausch Health's bid to extend the Xifaxan franchise via rifaximin SSD failed. Both RED-C-3131 and RED-C-3132 trials missed their primary endpoint: delaying first-onset HE in cirrhosis patients with no prior HE history. 1,000+ patients, 398 sites, 17 countries. Well-tolerated but not effective vs. placebo. CEO Tom Appio reviewing full dataset. This leaves Xifaxan without a pipeline successor past the patent cliff.
